Introduction

 Abyss Watchers are a Boss enemy in Dark Souls 3. The soul, passing from one body to the next, forever warring, is one of the 5 Lords of Cinder.

 General info:

  • Health: 1548
  • Souls dropped after death: 18 000
  • Recommended Levels: 30 to 40
  • Drops: Soul of the Blood of the Wolf

 Weaknesses/Strenghts/Immunities:

  • Weak against: Lightning
  • Resistant to: Dark, Bleed
  • Immune To: Poison/Toxic
