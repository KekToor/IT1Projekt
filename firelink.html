Introduction

 Firelink Shrine is a Location in Dark Souls 3. After defeating the first boss, the unkindled find this safe haven, where they may level up via the Fire Keeper, or interact with other NPCs who have sought the safety of the hub.

 General info:

  • Previous: Cementery Of Ash
  • Next: High Wall Of Lothric
  • Recommended Levels: Any
  • Bosses: None
  • Bonfires: Firelink Shrine
